Top 10 Android Apps That Pay Real Money (2025)

Looking to make extra cash using just your phone? In 2025, Android users have more opportunities than ever before to earn real money through apps that reward them for completing tasks, watching videos, playing games, and more. Here's a list of the top 10 apps that actually pay — all tested and trusted.

1. Swagbucks

Swagbucks pays you to complete surveys, watch short videos, shop online, and more. You earn "SB" points, which can be redeemed for PayPal cash or gift cards. It's one of the most popular reward platforms and is trusted by millions.

2. Mistplay

If you love mobile gaming, Mistplay rewards you for trying new games. You earn units based on how much you play, which can be redeemed for gift cards. It’s an easy way to monetize your gaming habit.

3. InboxDollars

Similar to Swagbucks, InboxDollars pays you to read emails, complete surveys, and play games. You can cash out once you reach $30. It has been around for over a decade and has paid out millions to users.

4. Toluna Influencers

This app allows you to influence big brands by sharing your opinion. You earn points from surveys, which can be exchanged for PayPal or other prizes.

5. Google Opinion Rewards

Backed by Google, this app sends you quick surveys and gives you Play Store credit or PayPal money in return. It's simple, quick, and ideal for earning on the go.

6. Field Agent

Get paid to do mini missions like checking product displays or taking photos in stores. Perfect for people who are always on the move. Tasks usually pay between $3 and $12 each.

7. Sweatcoin

This fitness-based app pays you in Sweatcoins for walking. You can use the coins for rewards, services, or donate them to charity. Some promotions allow conversion to cash.

8. Rakuten

While not a survey app, Rakuten (formerly Ebates) gives you cashback from thousands of online stores. Cashbacks go directly to your PayPal account or via mailed check.

9. Current Rewards

Earn cash just by listening to music! You can also watch videos and do daily tasks. Payouts are through PayPal and can be surprisingly good for minimal effort.

10. Survey Junkie

Known for its higher-paying surveys, Survey Junkie lets you earn $5–$50 by answering market research questions. Cash out is available from just $5 via PayPal.

Each of these apps is legit and pays in 2025. Combine several to boost your income stream and turn your phone into a money-making machine.
